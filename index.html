About the Lab

Intelligence in service of everything that matters

Onemee Laboratory is an independent AI research group operating at the intersection of machine learning theory and grand scientific challenge. We pursue research driven by curiosity and impact.

Our work spans reinforcement learning, large language models, and large reasoning models — applied to science, safety, and cosmic discovery. We collaborate internationally across Asia, Europe, and North America.

Our Philosophy

Independent research.
Outsized impact.

We believe the most important AI research can be done independently — outside the constraints of large corporate labs. Small, focused teams with deep mathematical foundations can produce work that changes the trajectory of the field.

Every project connects to at least one of our three pillars: making AI safer, accelerating science, or expanding our cosmic knowledge.

Open to PhD candidates and visiting researchers
Strong mathematical foundations required
International collaboration welcome
