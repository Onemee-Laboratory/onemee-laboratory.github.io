Mission

Intelligence in service of everything that matters

Onemee Laboratory is an independent AI research group at the intersection of machine learning theory and grand scientific challenge — pursuing research driven by curiosity and the desire for lasting impact.

We operate outside the constraints of large corporate laboratories. Small, focused teams with deep mathematical foundations can produce work that changes the trajectory of the field.
